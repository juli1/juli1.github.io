Time to go

It is now time to take a real break. From running races, social media and all the technology. It is not a way to throw everything away (you cannot change who you are) but is rather an opportunity to find new outlets, take some fresh air, be out of my comfort zone, explore new things, take new challenges. This is the right time to do it. Leave the useless, just keep the best and focus on what really matters.

If you are stupid enough to contact me, you probably know how to do it and if not, my contact information are online. I might post some stuff here to keep the feeling that I am in, alive and connected. And as some old friends used to say, a good song is better than thousands of words.

Ciao & see you soon.
